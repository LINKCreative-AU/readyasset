How do business loans work?

Updated 8 July 2026

A business loan advances your business a lump sum or credit line repaid from trading income: you apply once, we run the scenario across 45+ lenders — banks, fintechs and specialist funders — and you get your best options from the panel, often with approval the same day. The initial quote doesn’t impact your credit score, and low-doc options exist for businesses without full financials.

The panel covers unsecured business loans (fast, no property security), secured facilities, lines of credit and invoice finance through funders like ScotPac and Earlypay. What fits depends on how the money works in your business — a one-off purchase, a seasonal buffer, or freeing up cash sitting in unpaid invoices.

Lenders mainly look at time trading, ABN and GST registration, and turnover. Most fintech lenders want 6–12 months trading and can fund within days; banks price keener on longer histories. We’ll place you where your profile lands strongest.

Business finance questions, answered.

The things people ask us every week about business loans.

What do I need to qualify for a business loan?

Typically an active ABN, 6+ months trading (12+ opens more doors), and turnover the repayments fit inside. GST registration and property backing widen the panel further — but neither is mandatory with the fintech lenders.

Can I get a business loan without financial statements?

Yes — low-doc products assess on business bank statements instead of formal financials. Expect slightly higher pricing in exchange for speed and simplicity; often that trade is worth it for short-term needs.

Unsecured or secured business loan — what’s the difference?

Unsecured needs no property security, approves fast, and prices higher; secured borrows against property or business assets at lower rates. The panel quotes both where eligible, so you compare real numbers, not theory.

How fast can a business loan be funded?

Fintech lenders on our panel commonly approve same-day and fund within 24–72 hours. Bank facilities take longer but price sharper — we’ll tell you honestly which path fits your timeline.

What is invoice finance and is it on your panel?

Invoice finance advances you most of the value of unpaid invoices instead of waiting 30–90 days for customers to pay. Specialists like ScotPac and Earlypay on our panel do exactly this — useful when growth is eating your cash flow.

Does applying affect my credit file?

The quick quote doesn’t touch your file — it’s a panel assessment. A credit check happens only when you proceed with a chosen lender, and we’ll flag it before it happens.

Can a new business get a loan?

Under 6 months’ trading is hard for most lenders, but not always impossible — strong contracts, industry experience or security help. If it’s genuinely too early, we’ll say so and map what unlocks at 6 and 12 months.

How do repayments work on fintech business loans?

Many charge a fixed fee and collect daily or weekly rather than monthly — which suits businesses with daily takings. We translate every offer into a total cost of funds so you can compare it against bank pricing like-for-like.

Will applying affect my personal credit score?

The quick quote doesn’t touch personal or business files. Directors’ personal credit is usually checked at formal application for smaller companies — we’ll flag exactly when that happens before it does.
